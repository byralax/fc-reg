FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the digital soft copy legally valid?
Yes! Our digital soft copies are FRSC-approved temporary proof of registration. They're valid for 30 days until your physical plates arrive.
How fast is "same day" service?
Most requests submitted before 2pm local time are processed the same business day. Evening and weekend submissions are processed the next business day.
What documents do I need to provide?
Typically you'll need your driver's license, proof of insurance, and vehicle title or current registration. We'll guide you through exact requirements.
Can I get specialty or personalized plates?
Absolutely! We process all standard and specialty plate requests. Additional fees may apply for personalized plates.
What if my registration is expired?
We can help with late renewals and often minimize or eliminate late fees. Contact us immediately to discuss your situation.
How do I get my physical plates?
Physical plates are mailed directly from the FRSC to your address on file, typically within 5-7 business days after processing.
